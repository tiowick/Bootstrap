To understand the concept, one can look at what we understood as media before the existence of the internet: radio, TV, newspapers, magazines. When media became available on the internet, it ceased to be static and started to offer the possibility of interacting with other people.

Personalization of the message and direct interaction with the customer: on social networks, it is possible to have a much more personalized and direct relationship with each customer or potential customer, as you can get in touch with each one, whether to solve problems or discover new ones.
